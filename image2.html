Miss Rose Lipstick

Description:

Elevate your makeup look with Miss Rose Lipstick, a collection of luxurious lip colors designed to make a statement. With a wide range of vibrant shades, from bold reds to subtle nudes, there's a shade for every mood and occasion. The long-lasting formula ensures that your lip color stays put throughout the day, so you can confidently go about your day without worrying about constant touch-ups. Infused with nourishing ingredients like vitamin E and shea butter, Miss Rose Lipstick not only provides intense color payoff but also keeps your lips hydrated and moisturized. Say goodbye to dry, cracked lips and hello to a smooth, luscious pout.


Key Features:


              Long-lasting formula
              Hydrating and moisturizing
              Intense color payof

              Vitamin E

Prep your lips by exfoliating and applying a lip balm.
            Apply Miss Rose Lipstick directly from the bullet or use a lip brush for more precision
