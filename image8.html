Miss Rose Eyeliner

Description:

Achieve precision and definition with Miss Rose Eyeliner, a long-lasting formula that glides on smoothly for a flawless finish. Whether you're going for a classic winged look or a trendy graphic liner, this eyeliner is your go-to for creating stunning eye looks.


Key Features:


            •	Long-lasting formula
            •	Glides on smoothly
            •	Provides precise application
            •	Intense color payoff
Miss Rose Eyeliner is available in a variety of shades, from classic black to vibrant colors, allowing you to express your creativity and 
style. The waterproof formula ensures that your eyeliner stays put all day without smudging or fading.
            
Infused with nourishing ingredients like vitamin E and jojoba oil, Miss Rose Eyeliner not only enhances your eyes but also nourishes and 
protects the delicate skin around them. The formula is gentle enough for sensitive eyes and contact lens wearers, making it suitable for everyone.

Ingredients:


            •	Vitamin E
            •	Jaboba Oil

How to Use:


Starting at the inner corner of your eye, draw a thin line along your lash line, gradually thickening as you reach the outer corner. For a 
winged look, extend the line slightly beyond the outer corner of your eye and angle it upwards towards the end of your eyebrow.

Experience the precision and versatility of Miss Rose Eyeliner. Whether you're a makeup pro or just starting out, this eyeliner will help 
you achieve your desired look with ease.
