Miss Rose Pore Refining Serum

Description:

Achieve smoother, more refined skin with Miss Rose Pore Refining Serum, a lightweight formula that helps to minimize the appearance of pores and improve skin texture. Formulated with ingredients that help to unclog pores and reduce excess oil production, this serum is perfect for those looking to achieve a clearer, more even complexion.


Key Features:


            Minimizes the appearance of pores
            Improves skin texture
            Helps to unclog pores and reduce excess oil production
            Lightweight formula
Miss Rose Pore Refining Serum is enriched with ingredients like salicylic acid, niacinamide, and witch hazel extract, 
which help to exfoliate the skin, reduce inflammation, and tighten pores. The lightweight formula absorbs quickly 
into the skin, leaving no greasy residue behind.
            
Infused with a refreshing fragrance, Miss Rose Pore Refining Serum leaves your skin feeling clean, refreshed, and 
revitalized. The serum is suitable for all skin types and can be used daily as part of your skincare routine.

Ingredients:


            •	Salicylic Acid
            •	Niacinamide
            •   Witch Hazel Extract

How to Use:


Apply a small amount of Miss Rose Pore Refining Serum to clean, dry skin and massage in gently until fully absorbed. Use 
daily for best results.

Experience the difference with Miss Rose Pore Refining Serum. Say goodbye to enlarged pores and hello to smoother, more 
refined skin.
